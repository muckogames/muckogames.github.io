## Cleaned story segment: "Sillies Compound Raid"

Okay, now we start the story.

Digory woke up in a supply cabinet inside the Sillies compound. The Sillies were another mischief-making group, and their compound was even bigger than the Don's.

First thing in the morning, Digory poured kibble from the Sillies pantry and ate breakfast.

Then Digory asked Johnny Mackerel, "You mapped the whole place, right?"

Johnny said, "Yes. I made a digital map after that day you yelled 'Holy mackerel!' and I thought you were calling me. I swam through ducts and vents so fast that I learned the whole compound."

Digory laughed. "Perfect. Where do we deeb next?"

Johnny replied, "The server room. That's where they keep sabotaged computer equipment."

"Great. What's the code?"

"3657."

Digory, Duck Dieb, and **Swan Dieb** ran to the server room and entered **3657**. Inside were piles of computers that the Sillies had taken after installing crashing software on them.

Duck Dieb and Swan Dieb stuffed computers into Deeb bags and shoveled loot out a window into the basket of their hot-air balloon.

At lunch they ate sandwiches in the balloon basket, then flew roughly a kilometer to another rooftop building where a swimming pool had gold coins at the bottom. They drained the pool, scooped up the coins, and even carried off the pool structure under the balloon.

Back at their hideout they unloaded contraband. Among the haul was a huge crate of wet dog food. Digory called it a jackpot and had a giant dinner portion.

Meanwhile Swan Dieb fired up a grill and cooked corn. A fuel leak started in the grill line, so they shut it down and called Fireplace Robot. Fireplace Robot repaired it, relit safely, and kept things under control.

Digory went to his room and curled up under his yellow blanket. A big fire sound boomed from outside. Digory called, "Need help?"

Fireplace Robot answered, "Don't worry, Digory. Big conflagration, but we're handling it."

Digory said, "I believe in you, Fireplace Robot," and went to sleep.

A moment later Fireplace Robot returned and reported, "It's out."
